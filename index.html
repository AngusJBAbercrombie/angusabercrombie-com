About

Serving Precinct 8 for three years

For the last three years, I've been proud to serve as your Town Meeting Member, representing our beautiful corner of Belmont. We need to maintain strong schools and services, keep Belmont affordable, and do it all with a government that respects and accepts your input.

I'm asking for your vote to continue working on new programs and reforms to achieve that vision. From unifying the town finance team to streamlining key permits, we've made progress that will yield small but meaningful benefits in the years to come. I'm committed to building on this work over the next three years — but I can only do it with your support.

Record

Real progress, measurable results

$600K

Unlocking funds through fiscal reform

In 2023, Angus filed an article to finally address a thirteen-year-old recommendation to unify the Town's Finance Team and bring accountability to the Board of Assessors. Through his advocacy, this measure passed Town Meeting and at the ballot box. With fresh leadership, the Board of Assessors identified $600,000 that could be returned to the town to support key conservation and recreation projects.

🌿

Protecting Belmont's environment

Angus was appointed to the Belmont Conservation Commission in July 2024. He is working to permanently protect Rock Meadow, advocate for clearer permitting standards, and protect accessibility across conservation lands.
