Announcement June 3, 2025

Re-elected to Serve Precinct 8 — Thank You

I'm honoured and humbled to have been re-elected as your Town Meeting Member for Precinct 8. This result is a reflection of your trust, and I don't take it lightly.

This campaign was about showing up — knocking on doors, listening to concerns, and making the case that local government can and should work better for everyone. That Precinct 8 sent me back for another term means the world to me.

The work we set out to do together isn't finished — and now we get to keep building on it.

What's next

Over the coming weeks I'll be sharing more details about the priorities I'll be bringing to Town Meeting this autumn, including continued work on permitting reform, conservation land protection, and fiscal accountability.

If you have ideas, concerns, or just want to talk, my door is always open. The best way to reach me is by email at angus@angusabercrombie.com or by phone at 617-584-9529.

Thank you, Precinct 8. Let's get back to work.
